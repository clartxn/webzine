BOSNIA

Me and my parents were in random countryside in Bosnia and got an Airbnb for the night. We arrived there late, like 11pm, and it was pitch black out. The gps told us to turn onto this like hilly dirt road with no lights and a small faded sign that pointed towards the name of the Airbnb. When we arrived at the house, no lights were on so we got out and opened the metal gate and walked towards the house with only our small phone flashlights giving us light. There was a swimming pool with no water in it and a cat moved in the yard and made a rustling noise which scared us. We went up to the door and rang the doorbell of the house but no answer. It felt like a scene of a horror movie, stuck in the middle of nowhere in the dark Bosnian countryside with no place to stay for the night and off the main road at this creepy house.
